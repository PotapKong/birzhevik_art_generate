## Visual Traits

- Angular S-like mark with folded ribbon geometry.
- White, black, and blue logo variants.
- Dark navy/black backgrounds with cyan and electric-blue glow.
- Large clean Cyrillic typography, usually white, with blue secondary line.
- Market context through candlestick charts, line charts, grid overlays, and glassy UI chips.
- Premium 3D finance objects: logo tokens, coin stacks, upward chart arrow, glass tiles, mechanical gripper, podcast microphone.
- Rounded rectangular media frames with generous radius.

## Initial Design Direction

The brand should feel like a premium financial media channel:

- analytical, not hype-driven;
- dark and cinematic, not generic corporate light UI;
- energetic through controlled cyan/blue glow;
- visual proof through market surfaces and objects;
- strict about fake market data and fake third-party logos.
