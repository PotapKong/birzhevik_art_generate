# Reference Gallery

Use this file as the text routing library for Birzhevik image directions. It does not replace visual inspection of `references/images/`.

Before generating, inspect:

1. `references/images/birzhevik-identity-page-1.png` for logo, typography, and palette.
2. One or more format-specific references:
   - page 2 for social header and video preview;
   - page 3 for token/finance object language;
   - page 4 for podcast cover;
   - page 5 for logo glow, wave, and background grid.

Choose the closest archetype:

1. Social channel header
2. Video preview / episode cover
3. Podcast cover
4. Token / coin premium object
5. Market education infographic
6. Brand identity hero

Do not mix more than two archetypes in one image.

## 1. Social Channel Header

Best for: Telegram, YouTube header, VK/community header, channel positioning.

Look:

- wide rounded banner;
- dark market background;
- large `Биржевик` headline;
- blue secondary line;
- glass topic chips;
- logo mark in a physical or glass object.

Avoid: too many chips, small captions, busy exchange dashboards.

## 2. Video Preview / Episode Cover

Best for: video lessons, market explainers, YouTube/Telegram previews.

Look:

- Birzhevik lockup top-left;
- large white headline, often left;
- short supporting caption;
- 3D token stack, chart, or market object on the right;
- dark blue glow near the bottom/right.

Avoid: fake YouTube buttons, tiny subtitles, overfilled broker screens.

## 3. Podcast Cover

Best for: podcast identity and episode art.

Look:

- microphone hero on right or center-right;
- large title on left;
- Birzhevik topic breadcrumb near top;
- audio waveform pill at bottom;
- dark market background with subtle candlesticks.

Avoid: multiple microphones, fake podcast platform UI, crowded episode notes.

## 4. Token / Coin Premium Object

Best for: premium thumbnails and evergreen market topics.

Look:

- one large metallic Birzhevik token or stack;
- cyan rim light;
- dark background;
- chart arrow or grid as support;
- clean headline zone.

Avoid: coin piles, cash rain, casino vibe, crypto meme visuals.

## 5. Market Education Infographic

Best for: basics, risk, asset classes, checklists, comparisons.

Look:

- 2-4 blocks max;
- exact numbers only from input;
- blue key takeaway;
- abstract bars or chart shapes instead of fake detailed UI text.

Avoid: dense tables and tiny sources unless the user provides a real screenshot.

## 6. Brand Identity Hero

Best for: brand announcements, cover systems, design tests.

Look:

- logo mark as the hero;
- simple dark gradient/grid background;
- minimal text;
- strong blue glow.

Avoid: generic logo mockups with no Birzhevik palette.
