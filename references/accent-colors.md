# Accent Color Discipline

Use the supplied identity PDF as the source of truth for Birzhevik colors.

## Palette

- Deep navy: `#00004A`
- Royal dark blue: `#01037A`
- Bright blue: `#0199F7`
- Cyan: `#02A5FF`
- Electric brand blue: `#0042FF`
- Gradient: `#02A5FF -> #0042FF`

## Priority

Use colors in this order:

1. Dark navy/near-black canvas.
2. White primary text.
3. Cyan/blue glow and brand gradient.
4. Muted chart/grid texture.
5. Small glass or metallic highlights.

## Main Rule

Blue/cyan is the primary brand accent. Use it for:

- logo gradient;
- token rim light;
- headline support line;
- glass chip glow;
- active chart/route;
- audio waveform/play icon;
- object edge light.

## Avoid

- green/red as the dominant palette;
- orange/yellow profit signals;
- purple SaaS gradients;
- cyberpunk magenta;
- casino gold;
- random extra accent colors.

Green/red may appear only as subtle chart data when real market context demands it, not as the identity palette.
