# Birzhevik Cover Patterns

Use this reference when a post needs a stronger visual metaphor.

## Editorial Extraction

Convert the content into:

- Topic: asset, market event, lesson, risk, podcast, or trading idea.
- Claim: what changed or what the author believes.
- Reader consequence: what the investor should notice or do differently.
- Tension: opportunity vs risk, signal vs noise, price vs value, emotion vs system.
- Visual metaphor: one object, chart, token, or audio/market scene.
- Thumbnail hook: why the image stops scrolling before the text is read.

## Prompt Details That Help

- "Use only 3 main visual elements."
- "Keep chart details abstract; no fake prices or tickers."
- "Use one cyan/blue glow source."
- "Make the Birzhevik token/object the hero."
- "The image should feel like premium financial media, not crypto hype."
