# Format Rules

Choose the target format first, then apply safe-zone and layout rules.

## 16:9 Telegram / Dzen / Article Cover

Goal: strong feed thumbnail.

- Headline occupies 30-45% of the canvas.
- Hero object or chart scene occupies 45-60%.
- Keep 6-8% safe margins.
- Use one main metaphor.
- Brand mark/lockup top-left or bottom-left.
- Dark background with chart/grid atmosphere.

## Video Preview

Goal: readable video thumbnail.

- Large headline on the left or center-left.
- Short subtitle only when needed.
- Hero object on the right.
- Birzhevik logo top-left.
- Avoid more than one small caption.

## Social Header

Goal: panoramic channel identity.

- Brand name large.
- One subtitle line.
- 2-4 topic chips max.
- Logo object or glass tags centered/right.
- Keep text away from profile-photo crop zones when known.

## Podcast Cover

Goal: square or wide podcast art.

- Microphone or audio object as the hero.
- Title large and uncluttered.
- Optional waveform pill.
- Birzhevik breadcrumb/lockup near top.
- Avoid small episode descriptions.

## 9:16 Reels / Stories Cover

Goal: readable vertical cover.

- Headline in top 35-45%.
- Hero object in middle.
- Brand mark in bottom safe zone.
- Keep key text in central 80% width.
- Avoid wide chart diagrams.

## Clean Financial Infographic

Goal: compact explanatory visual.

- One headline.
- 2-4 cards max.
- Exact data only from input.
- Use large numbers and short labels.
- Keep chart details abstract unless a real screenshot/data source is supplied.

## Outpainting / Crop Repair

- Preserve the original inner image.
- Extend dark gradient/grid background.
- Do not rewrite existing text unless requested.
- Keep brand glow consistent.
