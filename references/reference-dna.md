# Birzhevik Reference DNA

Use this file after inspecting the actual reference images. It captures the shared visual behavior visible in the supplied Birzhevik identity PDF.

## What The Best References Have In Common

- Dark navy/black financial canvas.
- Electric blue and cyan glow as the energy source.
- Large white Cyrillic headline with clean modern grotesk proportions.
- The official Birzhevik logo or lockup, when present, is based on a real reference from `assets/brand/` or `references/images/`. S-like geometry may appear only as an abstract token/object motif, not as a prompt-invented official logo.
- Market context is visible through candles, charts, line graphs, grids, or financial topic chips.
- A single strong 3D object carries the visual: token stack, coin, chart arrow, microphone, glass logo tile, or gripper.
- Rounded media frames and glass pills feel premium, not default SaaS cards.
- The image feels analytical and high-trust, not gambling, crypto-hype, or generic cyberpunk.

## Strong Archetypes

### Dark Market Banner

Use for channel headers, Telegram/Dzen covers, and market-wide topics.

Look: dark panoramic background, large brand name or headline on the left, candlestick/chart atmosphere, glass topic chips, real Birzhevik logo reference preserved without changes, and optional abstract token object in the center/right.

### Token Hero

Use for investment basics, market mechanics, trading ideas, portfolio/risk topics, and premium object covers.

Look: 3D Birzhevik coin/token/stack, blue rim light, dark background, subtle chart/grid texture, headline in a calm safe zone.

### Podcast Studio

Use for podcast covers, audio episodes, interviews, and commentary.

Look: large microphone as the hero, dark market background, Birzhevik lockup top-left, headline left/center, optional audio waveform pill.

### Glass Tag System

Use for topic taxonomy, rubrics, playlists, channel navigation, and social headers.

Look: glassy rounded chips with short labels such as `Биржевая торговля`, `Аналитика`, `Обзор акций`, `Торговые идеи`; keep chips sparse and large.

### Financial Infographic

Use for comparisons, risk notes, asset allocation, numbers, bonds vs stocks, and educational slides.

Look: dark or near-dark surface, 2-4 cards max, bright blue highlight for the key takeaway, exact numbers only when provided.

## Rejection Test

Reject and regenerate when:

- the result looks like a generic trading thumbnail;
- charts contain fake precise numbers or misleading labels;
- the Birzhevik mark is generated, distorted, or presented as official without a real `assets/brand/` or `references/images/` reference;
- the headline is too small or too long;
- the image uses green/red market colors as the main brand palette;
- the style becomes neon casino, cyberpunk, crypto pump, or luxury-black with no market context;
- the composition is only a background chart plus text, with no real hero/proof.
